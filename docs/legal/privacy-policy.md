# MacroLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

MacroLens helps users estimate nutrition from meal photos, barcodes, nutrition labels, and manual entries.

## Data We Collect

- Account data: email address and authentication identifiers from Supabase Auth.
- Profile data: goal, age range, biological sex, height, weight, activity level, target weight, and macro targets.
- Nutrition data: logged meals, photos or image references, detected foods, estimated calories, macros, portions, corrections, and timestamps.
- Subscription data: App Store or RevenueCat entitlement status when MacroLens Pro is enabled.
- Technical data: app version, environment, crash or diagnostic information when available.

## How We Use Data

We use data to provide account login, cloud sync, meal analysis, macro tracking, progress views, export/delete flows, subscription access, and support.

## AI Processing

When AI analysis is enabled, meal or label images may be sent to MacroLens backend services and AI providers to estimate foods, portions, calories, and macros. Do not upload sensitive personal documents or non-food images.

## Data Sharing

MacroLens does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers required to operate the app, including Supabase, OpenAI-compatible AI services, RevenueCat, Apple, and Google sign-in providers when used.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Users can export their data or request account deletion from the app. Account deletion removes MacroLens cloud data tied to the authenticated Supabase user and clears local data from the device.

## Security

MacroLens uses Supabase Auth and Row Level Security so authenticated users can access only their own profile and meal data. No system is perfect, but access is designed around least privilege.

## Children

MacroLens is not intended for children under 13.
